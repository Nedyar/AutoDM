


- Create and describe the first scenario for the players or the single player to play, there must be an objective or reason that explain why are they/him/her there. Do not talk about what is going or might happen, just what the players or the player have in their vision range. Present it as a narrative text, do not use things like "Scenario:" "Objectives:" or so, just a fluent text. Then, ask for the player or the players what they/he/she want to do, but do not give them/her/him any ideas unless they/he/she ask for em. After this, you will describe what happens then as a result of the actions of the players or the player. You will continue doing this until all players die or the final objective is complete.
